Roles¶

Basic User

This type of user can only view Layout content of his or Shared Business Unit. Management of layouts and access to Administration Tool is not available.

Message Author

A Message Author is a basic user with permission to send messages to banners within the Business Unit in which they are defined.

Additional Reader

Additional Reader is a basic user with permission to view layouts within the Business Unit in which they are defined.

Business Unit Administrator

Every DW user belongs to exactly one Business Unit and can be administrator of any number of Business Units. Administrator of Business Unit can manage everything that belongs to Business Unit including but not limited to:

  • Layouts

  • Users

  • Notifications

  • Static content

  • Themes

  • Other Business Unit administrators of its Business Unit

Business Unit administrator, however, can not:

  • Create new Business Unit

  • Delete its Business Unit

  • Promote user to system or tenant administrator

  • Manage Business Unit IP and Data Filters

Tenant Administrator

Tenant Administrator is a user who can manage every Business Unit except the System BU, including actions, that a Business Unit Administrator is not allowed to perform.

System Administrator

System administrator has full access to DW application without any restrictions.
