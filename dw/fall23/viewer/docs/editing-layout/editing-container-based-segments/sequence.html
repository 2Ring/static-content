In Sequence, only one Item is displayed at a time. The Items are automatically alternated through.

For general information about this segment, see Sequence chapter.

  • Appearance

    The style of a Sequence

    • Smooth Transition

      Controls the transition, between two items. When disabled, the transition is instant and no animation is played. Otherwise, transition animation occurs between every two items.

      • Transition Speed

        Required
        Speed of the transition, selected from the predefined speed options
        Available options are Very Slow, Slow, Normal, Fast and Very Fast

      • Direction

        Required
        Direction of the transition, selected from the predefined direction options.
        Available options are Right To Left, Left To Right, Bottom To Top and Top To Bottom

  • Items

    Manually orderable
    List, displaying all items contained in the Sequence. A corresponding icon is displayed in front of each item, representing its type. An item without a selected type is represented by a question mark icon.

    Items are displayed in the order, determined by the Items list. The list is orderable, meaning the order can be manually changed.
